Our Variety of Netting

Fiberglass Wire Mesh

Fiberglass mire mesh is most economic type wire mesh, and because price reasonable, it most popular product. Black and grey color are available, screen size is 18x18 with soft surface, provide 95% ventilation. * For normal prevent insect and mosquito * suitable for: Magnetic screen, Basic Aluminum screen window and door, vertical Slide Type.

Aluminium wire mesh (0.18mm)

Aluminium wire mesh belong to traditional type insect screen, more than 30 years history in the country. Come with silver and black color, mesh size is 18x18, made by aluminium so it does not rust and good for ventilation. Keep 95% ventilation. Suitable for: basic aluminium insect screen window and door

Soft stainless steel wire mesh (0.6mm)

Material is soft 304 stainless steel wire, mesh size is 20x20, it is more durable than Aluminium Mesh. Provide 90% ventilation. * For prevent insects, rats and pets. * Suitable for basic aluminium window and door.

Polyester PET mesh

Material is polyester mesh, very durable and long lasting. Highly recommend for pet’s owners, this insect screen can last pet scratches. Provide 90% ventilation. Size are 16x18. * For prevent insects, rats and pets. * Suitable for magnetic screen, basic aluminium window and door.
