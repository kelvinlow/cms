Aluminium Panel Insect Screen

The most popular mosquito screen in Malaysia market

Once installed, it will deliver years of performance without any expensive maintenance.

Features:

  • High Quality Aluminium profile, good tensile strength
  • Smooth sliding with strong wheel system
  • Easy take out and wash

Various type of installation for aluminium systems:
Fixed panel, clip on, sliding, hinged/swing opening.

All of our Aluminium Systems are Custom Made to fix for installation
on both Inside and Outside of the house.

For sliding screens have a complete outer frame which the sliding panels are installed into, this will have a complete seal. The panels can be removed for cleaning by simply lifting out. It can be used fibre glass mesh, polyester mesh, stainless steel mesh or aluminium mesh.

Clip on screens are made with an aluminium frame with a fibreglass mesh/aluminium mesh. The pliable rubber gasket which holds the mesh in place is designed to keep the mesh secure and tighten. Screens are kept in place by retaining button clips and can easily be removed to clean.

Hinged Screen door can be made to fit most doorways both internally or externally. The door is held closed by a strong magnetic catch, and covers with fibreglass mesh/aluminium mesh.
